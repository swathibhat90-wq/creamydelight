About Us

From a Home Kitchen to Mangalore's Favorite Cakes

What started as baking for family and friends has grown into Creamy Delight - a home bakery dedicated to turning your celebrations into sweet memories. Every cake is baked fresh to order, designed around your theme, flavor and budget, and finished by hand.

From dreamy wedding tiers to fun birthday smash cakes and everyday cupcakes, each order is treated like a centerpiece worth celebrating.
